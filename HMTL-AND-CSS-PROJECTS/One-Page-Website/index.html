Gundam Card Game

Welcome to the Gundam Card Game! This is a simple card game based on the popular Gundam franchise. Players can collect and battle with their favorite Gundam cards. Stay tuned for more updates and features!

Rules

The rules of the Gundam Card Game are simple. Each player starts with a deck of cards and takes turns drawing and playing cards. The goal is to reduce your opponent's health points to zero. Each card has its own unique abilities and stats, so strategize wisely!
